About Us

We are a Dynamic Team of Women Advocates

SheSafeFoundation is an online community comprising of concerned young women whose services are to edicate women and children on domestic violence, sexual violence and the issues of rape and consent. we believe our services mus be inclusive and equitable for all individuals accessing our programs while being responsive to issues of race, gender, age and sexual orientation.

More Details Success Stories

Our Mission

SheSafeFoundation comprises of concerned young women whose services are to educate women and children on domestic violence, sexual violence when to give consent. Most importantly provide resources for them to know and understand their rights. To provide confidential services to victims of domestic violence, sexual assault and related form of violence.

Our Vision

Ensuring a society free from gender based violence with women and children. To build a society free of rape and sexual violence.

Our Services

Equality

The root causes of violence against women are societal, and the solutions lie in changing values, attitudes, behaviours, structures and systems to achieve gender equality advocates for equity and social justice in an effort to addresss and eliminate barriers to inclusion.

Partnerships

SheSafeFoundation will work in consultation, collaboration and partnerships, seeking input and sharing information to develop strategies and actions to advocate for and work towarads ending violnce against women and children.

Change making

SheSafeFoundation believes in the possibility for change in attitudes, behaviours and systems towards gender based violence, rape and consent.

Education

SheSafeFoundation supports and creates opportunities for education. we will share our skills, knowledge history and understanding about the causes and solutions to ending gender based violence and rape against women and children with our community.

Intersectionality

SheSafeFoundation will recognize and respect each woman's unique lived experience and her/their individual and multiple relationships to: ancestry, culture, ethnicity, gender, gender identity, language, physical and intellectual ability, race, religion, sex, sexual orientation, socio-economic status, record of offences, or receipt of social assistance.

Safety

All women and children have the right to live in saftey and dignity. SheSafeFoundation is commited to creating environments where all women and children feel safe. We build safety through healthy practices of inclusion, respect, self-care and confidentiality.

Equity

Every woman and child will be treated equitably at SheSafeFoundation. in order to increase accessibility and equity, a broad range of innovative and differentiated programs and services will be developed and offerd based on individual and community needs.
